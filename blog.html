Tips and tricks on breaking the ice

Lilly Matthews, June 27, 2019

Finding it hard to connect with the people you work with?

Being new to a city can be challenging, especially if you're new to Seattle. Don't fret though, we're here to help with these 5 tips on breaking the ice and making new friends, or simply becoming close with old friends.



1. Don't be afraid to ask your peers questions if you're stuck on a problem at work!

Research shows that your peers will almost never say no if you need help, and in fact, would love if someone asked them for help! Just make sure that you find the right time to seek assistance.
